> Minimum RTO for a single region outage Upto 20 minutes (As it will switch to read replica in another region)

> Minimum RPO for a single AZ outage: 0 (As Multi-AZ suport is setup)

> Minimum RPO for a single region outage: 0 (Read replication is setup in secondary region)
